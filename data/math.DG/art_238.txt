We calculate the Hirzebruch $\chi_y$ and $\hat{\chi}_y$-genera of symmetric
products of closed complex manifolds by the holomorphic Lefschetz formula of
Atiyah and Singer \cite{Ati-Sin}. Such calculation rederive some formulas
proved in an earlier paper \cite{Zho} by a different method.
