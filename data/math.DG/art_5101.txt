Sharp upper bound for the first eigenvalue
Let $M$ be a closed hypersurface in a noncompact rank-1 symmetric space
$(\bar{\mathbb{M}}, ds^2)$ with $-4 \leq K_{\bar{\mathbb{M}}} \leq -1,$ or in a
complete, simply connected Riemannian manifold $\mathbb{M}$ such that $0 \leq
K_{\mathbb{M}} \leq \delta^2$ or $K_{\mathbb{M}} \leq k$ where $k = -\delta^2$
or 0. In this paper we give sharp upperbounds for the first eigenvalue of
laplacian of $M$.
